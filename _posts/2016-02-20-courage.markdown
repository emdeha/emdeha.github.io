date:   2016-02-20

No one's going to give you something you ain't got the courage to get.

Even if there's someone who's willing to do this, would you feel well by
knowing you can't get anything from life by yourself?  Knowing you have to
depend on something external for your success to happen?  Yes, there are quite
many people who's success is only based on luck.  But luck ain't sustainable.
In the end, the chaos of life might get everything away from you and leave a
crumbling mess.  The chaos of life, however, can do the same with a person
who has realised their dreams with courage and dedication.  Humans feel
confident while they are around people with courage, though.  So, just for the
sake of not being alone, be courageous.
