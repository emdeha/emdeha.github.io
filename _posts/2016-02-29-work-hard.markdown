title:  "Measures of success"
date:   2016-02-29
---

Your measure of success is reality.  Feelings and emotions contribute to your
well-being and may make you more successful.  Feelings and emotions are not
objective, however.  You can't take imaginary and illusionary events and
deduce that you are successful because you are thinking of them.  You can only
be kind of sure you are achieving something if enough high-achieving peers of
yours are also acknowledging your effort and skills.  Given the fact that even
this measure of success is not reliable, anything less than it is subject to
fallacy.
