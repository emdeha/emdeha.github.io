title:  "Managing time"
date:   2016-02-10
---

Recently I found myself in many situations where I have much time on my hands
but don't have any meaningful project on which to put it to good use.  I'm
partially solving this problem by learning and doing side-projects.  However
interesting these activities are, they don't seem to bring me closer to my
purpose.  Actually, they do bring me closer to my purpose, the problem is that
they're not impacting many people.

I wonder what strategy should I employ in order to prevent such moments in the
future.  Or maybe I shouldn't employ any strategy at all...  These time spans
may be for the better.  They may be useful for my body and mind to recharge
itself---to fill-in with energy, mindfulness and knowledge and be prepared for
the moment when I won't have time even for the mere act of breathing.
